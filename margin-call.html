Margin &
Margin Calls

You do not pay the full contract value to enter a futures position. You post a margin deposit. Daily MTM losses erode that deposit — and when it falls too low, a margin call happens.

Distinguish contract value from margin deposit
Explain initial margin and maintenance margin
Identify what triggers a margin call and why
1
Activity 1 · Match the number
Which number is total exposure — and which is the deposit?
A futures contract shows these two numbers. Match each to the correct meaning.
₹12,42,500
₹1,20,000
Futures create leverage. You control ₹12,42,500 of exposure by posting only ₹1,20,000 — that is roughly 10× leverage. The margin is a performance deposit, not payment for the contract itself.
2
Activity 2 · Concept
Why is margin so much smaller than contract value?
Futures are leveraged instruments. The margin is not a down-payment on the contract — it is a buffer that the exchange holds to cover potential daily losses. This is why the exchange runs daily MTM: to ensure the margin buffer stays adequate.

Activity 4 · Margin call
Balance falls below maintenance. What happens now?
Initial margin = ₹1,20,000 · Maintenance margin = ₹90,000 · After Day 3 losses, balance = ₹84,000
A margin call is a risk control mechanism. When daily losses push the margin account below the maintenance margin threshold, the broker contacts the trader to top up the account. If the trader does not respond, the broker may square off the position to limit further losses.

The complete chain every student must know: Futures create leveraged exposure → you post a smaller margin deposit → daily MTM losses reduce the deposit → if it falls below maintenance margin, a margin call is triggered and you must add funds.
Margin & Margin Calls — remember this
Contract value = total exposure · Margin = the smaller deposit that supports that exposure
Initial margin is what you deposit at the start; maintenance margin is the floor
Daily MTM losses reduce the margin account balance
When the balance falls below maintenance margin, a margin call is triggered
A margin call requires you to add funds — or the position may be squared off
