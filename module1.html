Module 01 — Foundations

Foundations of
Derivatives

Learn the market grammar before the formulas. This module builds the language — spot markets, long and short, hedging, speculation, and arbitrage — through activities rather than text.

Explain what a derivative is and distinguish it from the underlying asset
Identify long, short, and squared-off positions correctly
Distinguish hedgers, speculators, and arbitrageurs by motive
Read and interpret a live NSE futures contract screen
