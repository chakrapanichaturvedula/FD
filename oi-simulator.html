Open Interest Simulator

Three traders — A, B, and C. Each round you decide who goes long, short, or flat and in how many contracts. Watch open interest update after every round and understand exactly why it moves.

How OI is computed: Open interest = total open long positions = total open short positions (they always match). When a new buyer and new seller enter → OI rises. When an existing holder closes their position → OI falls. A new buyer trading with an existing seller who is closing = OI stays the same.
